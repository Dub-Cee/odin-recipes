Freshly Cooked Jook

For centuries people in the asian communities, both rich and poor have been smacking that Jook

  • Rice
  • Chicken Stock
  • Shredded Chicken
  • Dried Bean Curd
  • Peanuts
  • Boil Chicken to both cook and create chicken stock
  • Remove chicken from stock pot and shredd
  • Add bouillon cubes to chicken stock
  • Add rice to chicken stock
  • Let simmer
  • Add chicen, peanuts, and dried bean curd to porridge
  • Let simmer until thick
  • Enjoy!
