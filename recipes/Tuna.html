Tuna Salad Sandwich

Tuna Sandwich w/Avocado

Aptly titled 'The sandwich of the Sea', the Tuna Salad sandwich swims head and shoulders above the members of the 'salad' sandwich genre

Ingredients

  • Canned Tuna
  • Mayonaisse
  • Dill Relish
  • Sourdough Bread
  • Avocado

Steps

  1. Combine tuna, mayonaisse, and relish in a bowl
  2. Toast sourdough bread to 6.5 out of 10 on the toastiness scale
  3. Spread tuna salad mixture onto toasted bread
  4. Top with sliced avocado
