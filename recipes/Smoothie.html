Strawberry Banana Smoothie

The most refreshing of all blended drinks: The strawberry banana smoothie has been a staple in the American household since long before man took to the skies

Ingredients

  • 1 Banana
  • 8 Strawberries
  • Pineapple Juice
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Add ingredients to a blender
  2. Blend
  3. Voila
